Our History

Since 2019, HI-Toyz has been selling plastic model kits and figure statues. Located in Jakarta Barat, HI-Toyz provides a selection of toys from various brands and has also become an activity center for toy collectors. HI-Toyz has a special place in the hearts of local collectors.
